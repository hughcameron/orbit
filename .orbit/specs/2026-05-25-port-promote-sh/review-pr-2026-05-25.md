The implementation respects the choice-0020 policy (CLI/MCP parity, internal-helper discipline, no orphaned wrapper at the named-artefact level), follows the precedent set by yesterday's 0.4.34 orbit-acceptance shim port, and lands all 11 ACs with concrete evidence.
